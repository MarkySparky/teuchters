    Register a croft

    The Crofting Commission regulates crofting in Scotland and operate the Register of Crofts.

    The Crofters (Scotland) Act 1993 requires the register to hold information on:

    • the name, location, rent and size of each croft
    • the name, age, and date of birth of the tenant and landlord
    • the address of the landlord
    • details of common grazing rights
